Task 5: Standards in Curation and Metdata

Metadata scheme

In my schema I have decided to include the following information:

  • Title: Title of my encoded piece
  • Composer: Composer of the piece.
  • Date Composed: Date on which the piece was composed
  • Album: Where does the song originate from? Is it a part of a larger whole? This is especially relevant for movie soundtracks to signify which movie did they come from.
  • Country Created: Country from which the piece originated.
  • Publisher: Publisher of the song. Also very relevant for movie soundtracks as it's also usually the producer of the movie.
  • Encoder: Person that encoded the file.
  • Encoding Date: Date on which the file was encoded.
  • Source: Source of the original document which was used for transcription
